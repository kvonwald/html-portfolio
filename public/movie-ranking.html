The Best Movies According to Kristin

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Against All Odds

Jeff Bridges at his peak. Loved the vibe of Mexico, living carefree in Mexico.

Thor: Ragnarok

I love the inspirational character of Thor, He is a hero, a god, and he doesn't know who he is. my favorite line is when Thor feels defeated because he lost his hammer, the source of his power; when his father tells him, You are not the god of hammers., and he realizes that he is the god of Thunder!

I Lost My Body

This is an animation film in french. This movie is so unique, it is filmed from the perspective of a severed hand. AN event which leads the main character through n extraordinary journey in self-discovery.
